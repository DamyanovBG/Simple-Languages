Simple-languages

Simple class file, that scans the ?lang= property on your website and defines variables you need on whatever languages you need.

Overview

Lets say you have a website for your project, that has many visitors from all around the world. You would like to translate your links, contact info or maybe the whole website. Its easy with this simple script. Just townload it and lets get started.

Installation

The installation is easy at 1, 2, 3.

1. Download from the links on the left or head over to my GitHub.
2. Extract the Languages.class.php to a deserved location on your website.
3. Add the following script to the beggining of all your pages you want to be translated.

include_once('/path/to/Languages.class.php');
$languages = new Languages('en');

Now you have installed the addon with a default language of English.

Adding Translation

Lets say you want to add some navigation links in English and Bulgarian. You can do that with the following script.

$en = Array(
'nav_home' => 'Home',
'nav_contact' => 'Contact',
);

$bg = Array(
'nav_home' => 'Начало',
'nav_contact' => 'Контакт',
);

We created the arrays with terms to add. Lets add them now.

$templates->add($en, 'en');
$templates->add($bg, 'bg');

To show your content, use this script.

echo NAV_HOME;
echo NAV_CONTACT;

Note the UPPER CASE.

Done!

Congratulations! You have successfuly installed the Simple Languages addon on your website! Now, everytime someone visits http://example.com/?lang=en or http://example.com/?lang=bg will see the result on the expected language.
